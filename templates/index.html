Welcome to Hatfield Junior Swimming School (HJSS)

Building confidence and skills in young swimmers, from beginners to advanced grades.

Our Programs

HJSS offers swimming lessons for children aged 4 to 11. Learners are grouped by grade levels from 0 to 5:

  • Grade 0: New to swimming
  • Grade 1-2: Beginner to basic swimmer
  • Grade 3-4: Intermediate swimmer
  • Grade 5: Advanced level

Each lesson is 1 hour long with a maximum of 4 learners per session.
